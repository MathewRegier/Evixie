About Evixie

Evixie is a digital marketing agency founded by Mathew Regier and co-founded by Dylan Schaafsma. It emerged from a critical question: Is the sophisticated technology that large Businesses use accessible for businesses of any size? This question led us down a path of development and research which culminated in the development of proprietary technology and software that provides advanced systems at an accessible cost, significantly exceeding our original projections.
